# The Yard

## Visual Description (for Image Generation)

**Setting:** Late medieval residential compound, upscale community (50x50 meters)

**Main Elements:**
- Ring of 10-12 multi-story houses arranged in rough polygon, facing inward
- Central well for clear water covered by 2-3 large shade trees
- Sitting area below the well with communal seating
- Southwest corner: stone hearth for communal cooking/barbecue
- Northwest corner: playground area
- Network of worn stone paths connecting all areas
- Four archway gates: Western Market Gate, Northern North Gate, Southern Beach Gate, Eastern Jungle Gate
- Grass covering most ground areas

**Visual Style:** Secure residential compound with functional communal zones

## Narrative Description (for Writers)

**Core Concept:**
Self-contained micro-community functioning as "safe container" for residents, especially children. Transitional space between private home safety and public world.

**Atmosphere & Function:**
- **Feeling:** Sheltered, communal, safe with constant low hum of community life
- **Passive Supervision:** Inward-facing houses create collective watchfulness
- **Sensory Layers:** Internal community sounds mixed with distinct gate influences:
  - Western Market Gate: commerce chatter, cooking smells
  - Northern North Gate: [to be defined]
  - Southern Beach Gate: sea spray, wave sounds, salt air
  - Eastern Jungle Gate: grass/wildflower scents, forest sounds
- **Scale:** Adult-small space becomes epic journey for child protagonist
- **Cultural Use:** Evening gatherings around central well, communal cooking at hearth, children's play in dedicated area
