# The Kitchen

## Visual Description (for Image Generation)

**Setting:** Late medieval kitchen, upscale family home

**Main Elements:**
- Large wooden worktable at standing height in center
- Stone hearth with iron cooking grate and chimney
- Built-in stone pantry shelves along one wall
- Hanging copper pots and wooden utensils from ceiling beams
- Clay storage jars and woven baskets
- Small window with wooden shutters
- Stone floor with drainage channel

**Visual Style:** Functional medieval cooking space with warm wood and stone materials

## Narrative Description (for Writers)

The kitchen serves as the heart of food preparation, designed for efficiency and communal cooking. The central worktable provides ample space for multiple family members to prepare meals together. The stone hearth radiates warmth throughout the room, while the pantry's cool stone shelves keep stored foods fresh in the tropical climate. 

**Key Details:**
- **Functionality:** Built for standing work and group meal preparation
- **Atmosphere:** Warm, busy, filled with cooking aromas and family conversation
- **Sensory:** Crackling fire, sizzling food, clatter of utensils, herb and spice scents
