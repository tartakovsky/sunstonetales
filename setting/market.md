# The Neighborhood Market

## Visual Description (for Image Generation)

**Setting:** Late medieval marketplace, upscale neighborhood

**Layout:** Open plaza with stone pavers, bordered by golden sandstone buildings with carved-wood balconies

**Structures:** Freestanding tropical hardwood stalls with colorful canvas awnings stretched overhead creating shade patterns

**Population:** Cat-like inhabitants around stalls

## Narrative Description (for Writers)

**Atmosphere:** Social hub rather than pure commerce - communal and integrated into daily neighborhood life

**Sensory Details:** 
- Thick, humid air
- Layered scents: cinnamon/cardamom (spices), grilling meat and fish smoke, sweet jungle fruits/flowers
- Constant energetic hum of animated, expressive voices and conversational haggling

**Cultural Notes:** Organized open space (not maze-like), where socializing and trading blend seamlessly
