**Concept 2: "I want to keep it AND trade it"**

**Behaviors/Skills to Teach:**

1. **Decision-Making Process**
   - Taking time to think before deciding
   - "Let me hold both and see how I feel"
   - Weighing pros and cons in simple terms

2. **Understanding Permanence**
   - "When I trade, it means goodbye to my fox"
   - Practicing with smaller decisions first
   - "This choice can't be undone"

3. **Values Clarification**
   - "What matters most to me right now?"
   - "Do I love my fox because I made it, or because I play with it?"
   - Distinguishing between attachment and actual use/joy

4. **Compromise Strategies**
   - "Can I trade for something similar?"
   - "Can I make another fox later?"
   - Finding creative solutions to the dilemma

5. **Acceptance Skills**
   - "It's hard to choose" - validating the difficulty
   - "I can't have everything, and that's okay"
   - Grieving what we give up while celebrating what we gain

6. **Future Planning**
   - "What will I make next time?"
   - Learning from the experience for future decisions
   - Building tolerance for temporary disappointment
