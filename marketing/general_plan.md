### Minimalistic Plan

#### English Market (Primary Focus: Sunstone Tales)
- **Legal Base**: Operate under EU legal entity for all formal business and monetization.
- **Branding**: Use `sunstonetales.com` and `@sunstone_tales` across platforms.
- **Content Types & Distribution**:
  1. **Story Videos**: Slightly animated, few-picture narrated videos (10 min) on YouTube only.
  2. **Narrated Podcasts**: Audio from story videos on major podcast platforms (Spotify, Apple Podcasts, etc.).
  3. **Plain-Text Stories**: Text-only versions for potential future use (e.g., website).
  4. **Few-Illustration Stories**: Publish to Amazon Kindle.
  5. **Fully Illustrated Books**: Publish to Amazon Printing.
  6. **Work-in-Progress Stuff**: Pictures and discussions (sketches, ideas) on Instagram.
  7. **Teaser Videos**: Punchy 15-sec teasers (2-3 animated pictures, dramatic voiceover) on TikTok, Instagram Stories, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts to drive traffic to YouTube.
  8. **Coloring Books**: Sell line-art versions of print book illustrations on Amazon Kindle and Printing (as separate titles or bundles).
- **Social Media & Community**:
  - **TikTok (@sunstone_tales)**: Teaser videos for story discovery.
  - **Instagram (@sunstone_tales)**: Teaser videos (Stories, Reels) and work-in-progress content (posts, discussions).
  - **Discord**: Build a community for fans to discuss stories and engage directly.
  - **Pinterest (@sunstone_tales)**: Post separate book illustrations and art, linking to `sunstonetales.com` for traffic to books.
- **Website**: Central hub at `sunstonetales.com` with mailing list subscription, linking to all platforms (YouTube, Amazon, social media).
- **Monetization**:
  - **Amazon Sales**: Revenue from Kindle and Printing (USD/EUR).
  - **YouTube Ads**: Monetize story videos (USD/EUR).
  - **Patreon**: Set up a Patreon account (@sunstone_tales) for exclusive content, early access, or supporter perks (USD/EUR).

#### Russian Market (Secondary, Diaspora Focus: Сказки Сарабада)
- **Legal Constraint**: No sales in RUB or direct engagement with Russia. Focus on diaspora via global platforms.
- **Branding**: Use “Сказки Сарабада” in metadata/playlists on shared English platforms. No separate domain or social media accounts.
- **Content Types & Distribution**:
  1. **Story Videos**: Slightly animated, few-picture narrated videos (10 min) on same YouTube channel (in “Russian Stories / Русские Сказки” playlist).
  2. **Narrated Podcasts**: Audio from story videos on same major podcast platforms.
  3. **Few-Illustration Stories**: Publish to Amazon Kindle (in Russian, for diaspora purchase in USD/EUR).
  4. **Fully Illustrated Books**: Publish to Amazon Printing (in Russian).
  5. **Teaser Videos**: Punchy 15-sec teasers on TikTok, Instagram Stories, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts (with language tags, e.g., #RussianStory) to drive traffic to YouTube playlist.
- **Telegram Channel**: Maintain a low-profile channel for free content distribution (stories, updates) to diaspora, avoiding formal marketing.
- **Website**: Optional Russian section on `sunstonetales.com/ru` if feasible, linking to Amazon and YouTube content. Keep minimal.
- **Promotion**: No direct promotion; rely on word-of-mouth via personal diaspora networks (friends, community groups on Facebook/Telegram outside Russia). Optimize metadata (e.g., “Русские детские сказки”) for organic search.
- **Monetization**: Passive via Amazon sales (USD/EUR only) if diaspora purchase. No active monetization push.
