# Food sets

*   Red Caviar: New Year's celebration; with butter on white bread or blinis, often with champagne.
*   Black Caviar: Formal occasion; on blinis with sour cream (smetana), or simply with a pearl spoon.
*   Kvass: Hot summer day outdoors; served chilled, on its own.
*   Pelmeni: Cozy family dinner; boiled and served with a large dollop of sour cream (smetana), black pepper, and fresh dill.
*   Smoked Fish: Casual get-together with friends; with black rye bread and beer.
*   Chocolate Bonbons: Afternoon tea time; served alongside a cup of hot black tea.
*   Pickled Cucumbers: A festive meal spread (zakuski); as a chaser for a shot of chilled vodka.
*   Canned Tomatoes: Dacha (summer house) meal; alongside grilled meats (shashlik) and boiled potatoes.
*   Zefir: Dessert after dinner; with unsweetened black tea or coffee.
*   Sushki: A simple snack at home; dunked in hot black tea to soften.
