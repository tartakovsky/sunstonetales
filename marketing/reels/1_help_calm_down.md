# Reel: How to help your child calm down

## Captions
- How to help your child calm down in 5 minutes
- Stay calm and supportive
- Sit with them
- Name their feeling: "You're angry"
- Validate it: "I would be angry too"
- Let them vent

## Animation Sequence
- 0-2s: Parent taking deep breath, staying composed
- 3-5s: Parent sits down beside upset child (getting on their level)
- 6-8s: Gentle hug/arm around child
- 9-11s: Parent speaking softly (speech bubble: "You feel angry")
- 12-15s: Child visibly relaxing, both calm together
