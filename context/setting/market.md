# The Neighborhood Market

## Visual Description (for Image Generation)

**Setting:** Late medieval marketplace, upscale neighborhood

**Main Elements:**
- Sun-drenched open plaza with stone pavers
- Golden sandstone residential buildings with carved-wood balconies as borders
- Freestanding stalls built from rich tropical hardwoods
- Patchwork of colorful canvas awnings stretched between stalls and balconies
- Shifting patterns of light and shade on stone floor
- Cat-like inhabitants gathered around stalls

**Visual Style:** Organized tropical marketplace with rich materials and vibrant awnings

## Narrative Description (for Writers)

The neighborhood market fills a sun-drenched open plaza, bordered by the golden sandstone walls of residential buildings. Instead of a maze of alleys, the space is organized around freestanding stalls built from rich tropical hardwoods. A patchwork of colorful canvas awnings is stretched between the stalls and nearby carved-wood balconies, creating shifting patterns of light and shade on the stone pavers below. **Key Details to Include:**
- **Setting:** An open plaza or square, not a labyrinth. It is bordered by the characteristic golden sandstone buildings with their carved balconies
- **Structure:** Freestanding stalls made of tropical hardwood. The shade comes from a patchwork of colorful canvas awnings stretched overhead
- **Sensory Details:** The mix of smells (spices, grilled meat, jungle produce) and the energetic hum of expressive, conversational haggling
- **Atmosphere:** Communal and lively, more like a social hub than a purely commercial zone. It feels integrated into the daily life of the surrounding neighborhood
