# Liska's Emotional Tells

## Negative Emotions

**Anger / Frustration**
- Paws clenched
- Eyes narrowed
- Teeth bared
- Sound: Low growl
- Sound: Hiss
- Tail: thumps the ground (image-only)

**Fear / Anxiety**
- Freezes in place
- Wide eyes
- Holds her breath
- Tiny shiver
- Sound: Whimper
- Sound: Gasp
- Tail: tucked between legs (image-only)

**Sadness / Sorrow**
- Shoulders slumped
- Eyes watery
- Lip trembles
- Head hung low
- Sound: Sniffles
- Sound: Long sigh
- Tail: lies limp on the floor (image-only)

**Confusion / Uncertainty**
- Head tilted
- Brow furrowed
- Mouth slightly open
- Looks back and forth
- Sound: Hmm?
- Sound: Murmur
- Sound: Puff of air
- Tail: forms a question mark (image-only)

**Overwhelm / Stress**
- Paws press over ears
- Shoulders hunch to ears
- Small, repetitive rocking
- Hides face in paws (but not hungs her head, no sadness)
- Eyes shut tight
- Winces (similar to pain)
- Body shrinks inward
- Sound: Small, repetitive whimpers
- Sound: Shaky exhale
- Tail: covers eyes (image-only)
- Tail: wraps tightly around body (image-only)

**Shame / Guilt**
- Eyes cast down
- Looks away, sideways, not meeting eyes
- Stares at the floor
- Head hung low
- Face hidden in paws, peeking out between fingers
- Sound: A quiet groan of regret
- Sound: A tiny, pained whimper
- Tail: tip covers her ears
- Tail: curled low, hiding her paws

**Boredom / Disinterest**
- Eye rolls
- Head propped on paw
- Looks at the ceiling
- Fidgets
- Sound: A puff of air ("Pfft")
- Sound: Long yawn
- Sound: A heavy, huffy sigh
- Tail: Sucks tail tip
- Tail: lies flat (image-only)
- Tail: tip flicks around (image-only)

**Helplessness / Powerlessness**
- Stares blankly at the problem
- Body goes limp, face falls
- Sound: A shaky, defeated sigh
- Sound: A small, quiet cry
- Tail: A small, defeated curl on the floor (image-only)

**Jealousy / Envy**
- Lower lip juts out
- Stares hard at the other, brows furrowed
- Glances sideways, eyes narrowed
- Sound: A quiet, sharp sniff
- Sound: A low, resentful grumble
- Sound: A small "tsk" sound
- Tail: nothing specific

**Disgust / Dislike**
- Nose wrinkled up
- Upper lip curled
- A slight, quick shudder
- Recoil
- Sound: "Eugh!", "Blech!"
- Sound: A sharp hiss
- Tail: recoils (image-only)

**Hurt (Emotional)**
- Hand flies to chest
- Wide eyes welling up
- Mouth slightly open in small shock
- Sound: A sharp, pained gasp
- Sound: A small, quiet whimper
- Tail: tucks in sharply (image-only)

**Loneliness / Isolation**
- Sits curled up small
- Stares into the distance
- Watches others from afar
- Sound: A long, slow sigh
- Sound: A quiet, lonely sniffle
- Tail: wraps around her body or lies curled small (image-only)

**Annoyance / Irritation**
- A quick, sharp glare
- Eyes roll upward
- Italian paw shake 
- Sound: A sharp, huffy sigh ("Hmph!")
- Sound: A low, impatient grumble
- Sound: A tongue-click ("Tsk!")
- Tail: whips back and forth or stiff (image-only)

**Indignation**
- Jaw drops open
- Eyes wide with shock
- Stares, unblinking
- Mouth forms an "O"
- Sound: A loud, sharp gasp
- Sound: A sputtering sound
- Tail: shoots straight up and puffs up (image-only)

**Disappointment**
- Face Melt: The bright, expectant expression slackens and falls.
- Short Freeze: Her body momentarily freezes in place, as if processing the shock of the loss.
- Sound: A sharp, quiet intake of breath

**Temptation**
- Leaning Forward: Her body leans slightly toward the object of temptation, as if drawn by an invisible force.
- Fidgety Paws (Reaching Slightly): Her paws twitch and reach slightly toward the object, showing the urge to grab or touch, then pull back as if remembering a rule.
- Wide, Fixated Eyes: Her eyes lock onto the object with an intense, unblinking stare.
- Tail Pointing Straight Out with a Bend: Her tail sticks straight out behind her with a sharp, excited bend at the tip, as if it's straining toward the object of desire, capturing eagerness in a funny, exaggerated manner.
