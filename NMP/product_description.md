# No Myth Parenting brand is
No Myth Parenting teaches parents how to raise emotionally healthy, resilient children using science-based methods. It provides practical tools and guides that help parents understand child development and respond to their children's emotions and behavior effectively.

# Products
## PDF Guide: How to deal with your child's big emotions

Visual style: line art illustrations.

What is it about: it teaches co-regulation for ages 3-7, 3R + Restore and so on. In our copy however we never state scientific terms unless specifically asked for. 

Table of contents. 
One-page guide gives you a short answer about what to do when your child melts down. If you do that, it will get better. Read the rest to understand why.
Part 1 gives a simple explanation of the psychology behind your child's behavior and emotions.
Part 2 introduces the classic science-based 3R approach: Regulate, Relate, Reason, extended with an additional step: Repair. 
Part 3 shows you 4 different ways children express overwhelm so you can recognize what's happening.
Part 4 gives you practical steps to start using co-regulation as easy as possible.
Part 5 covers essential self-care and quick techniques on how to stay calm.
Part 6 addresses common concerns and misconceptions about co-regulation.
Part 7 troubleshoots common problems you might encounter.
Part 8 explains when to seek professional support.
Part 9 contains additional strategies and tools.
References section contains scientific sources cited throughout the guide.
