# Rumbling Den: Emotional Sequence & Slot Analysis

This document outlines the emotional sequence of the Rumbling Den story, detailing the implementation of emotional tells and coping mechanisms for each act.

---

## Act 1: Setup – Establishing Normalcy

### 1.1: Playful Start
- **Emotion:** Joy
- **Slot Used:** Baseline happiness
- **Emotional Tell:** Smiling and active play
- **What Happens:** Liska arranges shells in the sunny corner of the den, engaging in imaginative play alone, setting a cheerful tone.

### 1.2: Happy Baseline
- **Emotion:** Contentment
- **Slot Used:** Family connection
- **Emotional Tell:** Relaxed body language
- **What Happens:** Liska plays happily, imagining beach adventures with family, feeling warm and secure in their shared space.

---

## Act 2: Inciting Incident – Discovery of Problem

### 2.1: Discovery of Tension
- **Emotion Triggered:** Confusion
- **Slot Used:** Goal Obstruction (understanding family dynamics)
- **Emotional Tell:** Furrowed brow, tilted head
- **Metaphor Used:** Tangled Yarn (Запутанный Клубок)
- **What Happens:** Liska enters the main den and notices her parents with stiff tails and serious faces, not looking at each other, creating a sense of unease.

### 2.2: Initial Reaction
- **Emotion Triggered:** Fear
- **Slot Used:** Uncertainty in safety
- **Emotional Tell:** Wide eyes, flattened ears
- **What Happens:** Liska feels confused and fearful about her parents' demeanor, unsure if the tension involves her, as she stands frozen observing them.

---

## Act 3: Attempts to Solve – Facing the Problem & Inner Turmoil

### 3.1: Attempt 1 (Initial Reaction)
- **Emotion Triggered:** Sadness
- **Slot Used:** Emotional Disconnection
- **Emotional Tell:** Drooping tail, slumped shoulders
- **What Happens:** Liska shows a shell to her father, hoping for engagement, but receives only a minimal response, feeling ignored as he remains distant.

### 3.2: Co-Regulation Scene
- **Support Type Used:** Emotional Validation
- **Slot Used:** Parental Guidance
- **Coping Strategy:** Listening and acknowledging feelings
- **What Happens:** Father explains they are having an important argument but still love her, offering reassurance while maintaining focus on their discussion with Mother.

### 3.3: Attempt 2 (Escalation)
- **Emotion Triggered:** Increased Sadness
- **Slot Used:** Persistent Disconnection
- **Emotional Tell:** Hiding face, quiet whimper
- **What Happens:** Liska peeks in later, sees her mother's wet fur from crying, and feels deeper sadness as the tension between her parents persists.

### 3.4: Low Point
- **Emotion Triggered:** Helplessness
- **Slot Used:** Performance Failure (inability to fix situation)
- **Auto-Coping Strategy:** Self-hugging with tail
- **What Happens:** Liska curls into a ball in the hallway, feeling helpless and unable to change the situation, overwhelmed by emotion as she hides alone.

---

## Act 4: Climax – Turning Point

### 4.1: Finding Solution
- **Slot Used:** Solution Discovery (emotional expression)
- **Learned Strategy:** Verbal expression of feelings
- **What Happens:** Liska remembers a family picture from a happy moment and decides to express her feelings to bridge the emotional gap between her parents.

### 4.2: Final Attempt
- **Emotion Triggered:** Hope
- **Slot Used:** Renewed Connection
- **Emotional Tell:** Brightening eyes, tentative smile
- **What Happens:** Liska brings the picture, takes her parents' hands, and expresses her love and desire for unity, urging them to remember their bond.

### 4.3: Breakthrough
- **Emotion Triggered:** Relief
- **Slot Used:** Family Unity
- **Emotional Tell:** Relaxed posture, tail wagging
- **What Happens:** Parents soften, apologize to each other and to Liska, and reaffirm their love, showing emotional reconciliation as a family.

---

## Act 5: Resolution – New Normal

### 5.1: Connection
- **Slot Used:** Re-established Bond
- **Emotional Tell:** Physical closeness with family
- **What Happens:** The family returns to the sunny corner, curling up together, physically reconnecting in their safe space.

### 5.2: Warm Celebration
- **Slot Used:** Shared Joy
- **Emotional Tell:** Laughter, group hug
- **What Happens:** The family shares a triple-kiss, a unique gesture of affection, demonstrating their unity and love.

### 5.3: Lesson
- **Slot Used:** Emotional Learning
- **Learned Concept:** Family conflicts are temporary, love persists
- **What Happens:** Liska internalizes the lesson that family dens may rumble but love is stronger than arguments, gaining emotional understanding.
