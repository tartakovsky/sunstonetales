## Act 5: Resolution & Conclusion - Integrating the Growth

### Beat 5.1: Reflection & Learning

### Beat 5.2: Making it Right / Integration / New Status Quo

"Morning, Mr. Cheesy." The plant shadow seemed to wave back. At breakfast, she announced, "Slothy was scared this morning, so we had a tiny dance party."

That night, when the shadows grew long, Liska snuggled with Slothy. Her heart might always beat a little fast when the shadows come out to play, but that was okay. She was a shadow dancer now. "Come on, Mr. Cheesy," she whispered. "Let's dance."
